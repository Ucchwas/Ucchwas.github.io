## Major Projects

### CardioHelp Health Monitoring System  
**Tools:** Android Studio, TensorFlow Lite, Kotlin  
- Edge AI mobile app for beat-by-beat ECG & respiration analysis, streaming via Bluetooth.  
- Achieved > 95 % accuracy, IRB-approved data collection, real-time clinician alerts.  
- [GitHub repo](https://github.com/cpslabttu/Smart-Health-Application-V4)  

### Respiratory & Beat Detection Algorithms  
**Tools:** Python, PyTorch, MIT-BIH datasets  
- DL & RL-enhanced algorithms to detect cardiac & respiratory events with 96.5 %+ accuracy.  
- Integrated into mobile app as TensorFlow Lite models.  
- [GitHub repo](https://github.com/Ucchwas/Apnea-ECG)  

### Inkjet-Printed Bioelectronics  
**Tools:** Dimatix DMP-2850, Ag191 ink  
- Fabricated flexible dry electrodes and supercapacitors on polyimide for wearables.  
- Single MIM capacitor > 1.2 nF; deployed in micro-robotics & IoT prototypes.  

### On-Demand Professor Q&A Bot  
**Tools:** Python, LangChain, ChromaDB, GPT-3.5  
- PDF ingest + vector retrieval to answer course material queries.  
- [Demo & code](https://github.com/Ucchwas/OnDemand-Professor-Q-A-Bot)
