## Bio & Vision

Healthcare is often reactive—critical conditions are caught too late.  
My research uses **cyber-physical systems** to shift to proactive, personalized care via:

- **On-device edge-AI**: Real-time ECG & respiratory anomaly detection with > 96.5 % accuracy  
- **Custom hardware**: Inkjet-printed flexible electrodes & supercapacitors for wearable comfort  
- **Mobile integration**: The CardioHelp app streams data over Bluetooth, runs optimized DL models, and alerts clinicians instantly
